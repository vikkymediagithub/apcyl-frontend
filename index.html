ABOUT US

Welcome to APC Youth League (APCYL)

The APC Youth League, founded by Comrade Atir Solomon Faeren on February 15, 2013, and officially recognized by the APC in 2014, is a dynamic organization rooted in the All Progressive Congress with structures ranging from the national level to polling stations.

Dedicated to socio-political engagement, political mobilization, and youth empowerment, the League plays a crucial role in fostering relationships between the party and its grassroots supporters.

  • ➜ Political Mobilization
  • ➜ Youth Empowerment
  • ➜ Leadership Development
  • ➜ Community Engagement
  • ➜ Policy Advocacy
  • ➜ Nationwide Reach
